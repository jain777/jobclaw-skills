# Action verbs (Harvard, organized by skill set)

Start every bullet with a strong, specific past-tense action verb, and **vary** them across the resume — never repeat the same verb twice on one page where avoidable. Pick the verb that matches the *kind* of work (build vs. improve vs. lead vs. analyze), then follow the formula:

> **action verb + what you did + quantified result**

Source: Harvard FAS Office of Career Services action-verb list (`ActionVerbsList.pdf` in the repo root).

## Anti-patterns (avoid as bullet openers)
- Weak/passive openers: *Responsible for, Worked on, Helped with, Tasked with, Involved in, Duties included*.
- First person (*I, my*) and articles (*A, The*) at the start.
- Vague verbs with no metric: *Handled, Worked, Did, Used* — unless paired with a hard result.
- Reusing the same verb across multiple bullets.
